Hi, I'm Ed

 

I’m currently based in Providence, Rhode Island, pursuing a Master’s in Industrial Design at the Rhode Island School of Design, after completing a Bachelor’s in Creative Media from City University of Hong Kong. My background is mixed, and so is my practice, which sits somewhere between art and design.

I’m drawn to political and social questions, to how systems shape people, and how design might challenge those systems instead of serving them. I don’t work in a fixed medium, and my practice spans product, graphic, systems, and concept design, often leaning toward the critical or speculative.

I’m also pursuing a side career as a tattoo artist.
